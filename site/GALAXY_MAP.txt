REGIONAL CHARACTERISTICS:

CORE (0-30 LY from Sol):
  • Controlled by: UEF (70%), ROM (30%)
  • Tech Level: 7-9
  • Population: Very High
  • Security: Maximum (Patrol Strength 7-8)
  • Trade: Stable prices, low margins
  • Features: Best shipyards, excellent outfitters
  • Danger: Minimal (safe for new players)

MID (30-60 LY from Sol):
  • Controlled by: FTG hubs (25%), UEF influence (25%), Independent (50%)
  • Tech Level: 5-7
  • Population: Medium-High
  • Security: Moderate (Patrol Strength 5-6)
  • Trade: Good opportunities, medium margins
  • Features: Trading stations, moderate selection
  • Danger: Low (occasional pirate activity)

OUTER (60-100 LY from Sol):
  • Controlled by: FWA (40%), Independent (50%), Crimson bases (10%)
  • Tech Level: 3-6
  • Population: Low-Medium
  • Security: Weak (Patrol Strength 3-4)
  • Trade: High margins, volatile prices
  • Features: Basic facilities, limited selection
  • Danger: High (heavy pirate presence)
  • Player Faction Zone: Primary area for player territory

EDGE (100+ LY from Sol):
  • Controlled by: Auroran Empire (100%)
  • Tech Level: 10 (alien technology)
  • Population: Unknown
  • Security: Maximum (Patrol Strength 9)
  • Trade: Very limited, exotic goods
  • Features: Alien stations, mysterious technology
  • Danger: Extreme (unknown threats)
  • Lore: First contact zone, mysteries

MAJOR TRADE ROUTES:

1. SOL (⊕) ══ Alpha Centauri (⊕) ══ Sirius (¤) ══ Outer colonies
   "The Main Line" - Safest and most traveled route

2. SOL (⊕) ── Tau Ceti (⊕) ── FTG Hub (¤) ── Frontier (⚑)
   "The Merchant Road" - High volume trading

3. ROM stations (♂) ── UEF space (⊕) ── FTG hubs (¤)
   "The Industrial Corridor" - Ship and equipment trade

4. FTG hubs (¤) ─ Frontier systems (⚑) ─ Edge (⧈)
   "The Explorer's Route" - Dangerous but profitable

5. Outer systems (·) ─☠─ Lawless space ─☠─ Black markets
   "The Smuggler's Run" - Illegal but lucrative

STRATEGIC CHOKEPOINTS:

• Sirius (¤) - Major FTG hub, controls trade to outer systems
• Epsilon Eridani (⊕/⚑) - Border between UEF and Frontier space
• Crimson bases (☠) - Control access to valuable outer routes
• Auroran border - Single entry point to alien space

PLAYER FACTION TERRITORIES:

Player factions typically claim systems in:
  • Mid-Outer border (best for new factions)
  • Outer independent systems (most available)
  • Contested FWA systems (with permission)

Strategic considerations:
  ✓ Proximity to trade routes
  ✓ Defensibility (few connections)
  ✓ Resources (tech level, population)
  ✗ Too close to Crimson bases (constant attacks)
  ✗ Too remote (no trade, no recruits)
